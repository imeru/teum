# TEUM

> Turn every gap into meaningful progress.

## Brand Philosophy

TEUM is not another to-do list.

It is a new way of thinking about time.

Life is made of gaps.

Five minutes before a meeting. Ten minutes while waiting. Twenty minutes
after lunch.

Most people lose these moments.

TEUM exists to help people discover them.

Every gap is an opportunity.

## What does "TEUM" mean?

"틈 (Teum)" is a Korean word meaning:

-   Gap
-   Space
-   Opening
-   Opportunity between moments

A gap is not empty.

A gap is potential.

TEUM transforms forgotten moments into meaningful progress.

## Core Belief

Small moments create big achievements.

One page. One email. One workout. One idea. One Pomodoro.

Repeated every day, they become something extraordinary.

Consistency is more powerful than intensity.

## Mission

Help people make meaningful progress, one gap at a time.

## Product Vision

TEUM combines:

-   To-do List
-   Daily Planner
-   Time Blocking
-   GTD
-   Pomodoro
-   Calendar
-   Micro Time Management

Instead of asking:

> What do I have to do?

TEUM asks:

> What can I accomplish with the time I have right now?
